Common Questions

Who truly owns the website?

You do. Once the final payment is settled, we initiate a full asset transfer. This includes the GitHub repository, hosting credentials, and all source code. You are never "locked in" to our service.

Why don't you use WordPress or templates?

Templates are often bloated with unnecessary code that slows down your site. We build custom HTML/CSS/JS to ensure maximum speed, security, and a 100% unique design that fits your brand perfectly.

What if I need updates after the project is done?

We offer flexible maintenance packages, or you can hire us on an ad-hoc basis for specific updates. Because the code is clean and standardized, any professional developer can also manage it in the future.

How does the real-time preview work?

We provide you with a staging link connected to your GitHub repository. Every time we commit a line of code, the staging site updates automatically, allowing you to see progress instantly from your phone or PC.
