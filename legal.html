Legal & Privacy

1. Service Transparency

JDR Corporation operates on a "Live Build" model. By engaging our services, you acknowledge that project progress is monitored via private GitHub repositories. Access is granted exclusively to the client and authorized JDR Corporation developers for real-time review.

2. Payment Terms & Intellectual Property

To ensure project commitment and resource allocation, JDR Corporation utilizes a 50/50 payment structure:

  • 50% Advance Payment: Required via GCash to officially kickoff the project, secure the repository, and begin architectural development.
  • 50% Final Payment: Required via Cash on Hand (or agreed method) upon the completion of the build and prior to the transfer of the full website account.

Ownership Transfer: Full intellectual property rights and code ownership are formally transferred to the client only after the final 50% payment has been settled. JDR Corporation retains the right to display the project in our professional portfolio unless a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) is in place.

3. Data Privacy

We prioritize your privacy. Any information shared via WhatsApp, Viber, or Direct Call—including phone numbers, personal identity, and project specifications—is used strictly for business transactions and is never shared with third parties.

4. Project Handover

JDR Corporation provides a seamless "handshake" service, ensuring your website is live on your chosen domain. Once the final account transfer is complete, the client assumes full responsibility for the security, hosting renewals, and maintenance of their digital assets.
