Terms & Conditions

The legal framework for our development partnership.

1. Ownership & Intellectual Property

Upon full payment, the Client gains 100% ownership of all website assets, including:

  • Custom code
  • Website architecture
  • Domain administrative rights

Domains are registered and funded by the Client, and JMCY WEB SOLUTIONS will never claim ownership.

JMCY WEB SOLUTIONS will not retain hidden access, proprietary locks, or backdoors. All digital assets fully belong to the Client.

2. Development Transparency

Projects are developed using private GitHub repositories. Clients receive viewer access to monitor code commits in real-time, development milestones, and project integrity.

3. Payment & Milestones

Payment follows a 50/50 structure:

  • 50% Upfront: To initiate project architecture and setup (domain must be registered prior to start).
  • 50% Final: Due upon project completion and Client approval.

Final payment is required before domain transfer, deployment, or launch.

4. Liability & Warranty

JMCY WEB SOLUTIONS provides work with professional care. We do not guarantee uninterrupted website availability or compatibility with all third-party services. Liability is limited to fees paid.

5. Privacy & Data Handling

All project-related information is kept confidential. Client data is stored securely and used only for project development. Data is never shared with third parties without consent.

6. Governing Law & Dispute Resolution

These terms are governed by Philippine law. Any disputes will be resolved in accordance with applicable regulations of the Philippines.

7. Termination & Refunds

The 50% upfront payment is non-refundable. If canceled mid-development, JMCY WEB SOLUTIONS will deliver any completed work up to the termination point so the Client retains ownership of what has been developed.

8. Support & Maintenance

Post-launch support is offered for an agreed period. The Client is responsible for maintaining domain and hosting services unless a separate agreement is in place.
