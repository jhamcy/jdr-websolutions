Legal & Privacy

Standard operating procedures and data protection compliance.

1. Service Transparency

JDR Corporation operates on a "Live Build" model. By engaging our services, you acknowledge that project progress is monitored via private GitHub repositories.

2. Payment Terms & Project Kickoff

To ensure project commitment and secure necessary digital assets, JDR Corporation utilizes the following payment structure:

  • Kickoff Payment (Advance): Includes 100% of the Domain Registration Cost plus 50% of the Service Labor Quote.
  • Completion Payment (Balance): The remaining 50% of the Service Labor Quote is required upon build completion.

Ownership Transfer: Full intellectual property rights and code ownership are formally transferred to the client only after the final payment has been settled.

3. Data Privacy (RA 10173)

In compliance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012, we prioritize the protection of your personal information. Any data shared is used strictly for project fulfillment.

4. Domain & Asset Responsibility

Domain registration prices are determined by the chosen extension and are billed at current market rates. The client assumes full responsibility for hosting renewals once transferred.

5. Liability & Technical Support

JDR Corporation provides 30 days of complimentary technical support post-handover to ensure your transition to the live environment is successful.
