Legal & Privacy

Standard operating procedures and data protection compliance.

1. Service Transparency & Project Abandonment

JDR WEB SOLUTIONS operates on a "Live Build" model. Project progress is monitored via private GitHub repositories to ensure total transparency. Project Abandonment: Projects inactive for more than 30 consecutive days due to client non-responsiveness will be deemed "On Hold." Reactivation may be subject to a rescheduling fee.

2. Payment, IP & Portfolio Attribution

  • Setup Phase: 100% of Domain Costs + 50% Service Labor is required to initiate. Domain registration fees are non-refundable once initiated.
  • Final Phase: The remaining 50% is due upon project sign-off.

Intellectual Property: Upon final payment, JDR WEB SOLUTIONS grants the client a perpetual, exclusive license to the final website code. JDR WEB SOLUTIONS retains the right to reuse non-proprietary code snippets and architectural patterns. Portfolio Showcase: The Client consents to JDR WEB SOLUTIONS featuring the project URL, screenshots, and associated Google Business reviews within our official Portfolio and marketing materials to demonstrate development capabilities.

3. Digital Asset Governance & Hosting

Concierge Hosting: If JDR WEB SOLUTIONS is contracted for hosting, we act as the administrative custodian to ensure uptime and security.
Management Transfer: The client may request a full "Management Transfer" at any time. We will transmit all master credentials (GitHub, Domain, Google Business/Ads) to the client within 48 hours of a verified written request.

4. Data Privacy (RA 10173)

In accordance with the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012, we implement strict measures to ensure business credentials and customer data remain confidential. All temporary development access is revoked and destroyed post-handover.

5. Liability & Dispute Resolution

Post-transfer, JDR WEB SOLUTIONS is released from liability regarding hosting uptime, server-side configurations, or security breaches resulting from client-managed credentials. Any disputes arising shall be settled through friendly negotiations. If unresolved, the venue for legal action shall be the courts of Manila, Philippines.
